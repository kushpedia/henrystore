Our Return Policy

At Kstores, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We want you to be completely happy with your purchase. If you're not satisfied with an item, we're here to help make it right.

7-Day Return Window

You have 7 calendar days from the date your order was delivered to request a return. We recommend inspecting your items immediately upon delivery.

Note: Returns requested after 7 days may not be accepted.

Eligibility Conditions

To be eligible for a return, your item must meet the following conditions:

Unused Condition

Item must not be worn, washed, or used

Original Tags

All tags must be attached

Original Packaging

Item should be in its original box/packaging

Proof of Purchase

Order number or receipt required

Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene and safety reasons, the following items cannot be returned:

  • Intimate apparel and underwear
  • Swimwear (if hygiene seal is broken)
  • Earrings and piercing jewelry
  • Gift cards
  • Final sale items (clearly marked)
  • Perishable goods
Please note: We reserve the right to refuse returns that do not meet our conditions.

How to Return an Item

1
Submit Return Request

Log into your account, go to your orders, and click "Request Refund" on the item you wish to return. Fill out the return form with your reason.

2
Wait for Approval

Our team will review your request within 24-48 hours. You'll receive an email notification once your return is approved.

3
Pack and Ship

Pack the item securely in its original packaging. Print the return label provided in your approval email and attach it to your package.

4
Drop Off

Drop off your package at any designated shipping location. Keep the receipt as proof of return.

5
Receive Refund

Once we receive and inspect your item, we'll process your refund. You'll receive a confirmation email when complete.

Refund Method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for purchase.

Processing Time

Refunds are typically processed within 1-4 business days after we receive and inspect your returned item. You'll receive an email confirmation once processed.

Return Shipping

We provide free return shipping labels for all approved returns. The cost of return shipping will be covered by us.

Restocking Fee

We do not charge any restocking fees. You'll receive a full refund of the item price.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately:

  • Email: support@kstores.com
  • Phone: +254 (703) 443-827
  • Response Time: Within 24 hours
Pro Tip: Take photos of any damage before returning. This helps us process your claim faster.

Exchanges

If you need a different size or color, the fastest way is to return the original item and place a new order separately. Once your return is processed, you'll receive a refund for the original item.

International Returns

For international orders, customers are responsible for return shipping costs. Please contact our support team for assistance with international returns.
