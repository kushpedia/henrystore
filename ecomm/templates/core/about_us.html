About KStores

Kenya's Premier Multi-Vendor E-Commerce Marketplace

Founded in 2025, KStores emerged from a vision to revolutionize e-commerce in Kenya by creating a unified platform where local businesses can thrive and customers can discover quality products with confidence.

We're more than just a marketplace - we're a community of passionate vendors, satisfied customers, and dedicated team members working together to build Kenya's most trusted online shopping destination.

The KStores Team

Our Mission

To empower Kenyan entrepreneurs with cutting-edge e-commerce tools while providing customers with a seamless, secure, and rewarding shopping experience.

  • Democratize access to digital commerce
  • Foster vendor success and growth
  • Build trust through transparency and quality

Our Vision

To become East Africa's most trusted digital marketplace, transforming how businesses operate and how consumers shop in the digital age.

  • Lead e-commerce innovation in East Africa
  • Support 10,000+ vendors by 2025
  • Create 50,000+ digital commerce opportunities
